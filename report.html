Official Reporting Channels

If you have been targeted by digital fraud, phishing, or social engineering in Ghana, it is crucial to report the incident directly to the relevant authorities. Choose the appropriate agency below to lodge your complaint.

Cyber Security Authority (CSA)

Available 24/7 to report incidents or seek advice regarding online threats and cybercrime.

Toll-Free / SMS 292 (All networks)
WhatsApp 050 160 3111
Email report@csa.gov.gh

Ghana Police Service

Report financial fraud, impersonation, and criminal activities to the dedicated anti-fraud hotlines.

Anti-Fraud Hotlines 0800 910 910
0547 41941

MTN Ghana (MoMo Fraud)

USSD Shortcut Dial *170# → 6 (My Wallet) → 4 (Report Fraud)
SMS Short Code Text fraud details to 1515
Toll-Free Helpline 100
Email mmfraudteam.GH@mtn.com

⚠️ Important Information

  • Official Calls: The MTN official fraud team calls come strictly from 0244300000.
  • Requirements: Provide the fraudster's number, name (if known), and a brief incident description.
  • Never share your PIN with anyone claiming to be MTN staff!

Telecel Ghana

Reach out to report network-specific impersonators or mobile money issues.

Helpline 050 555 5111
WhatsApp 050 100 0300
Email info@telecel.com.gh

National Communications Authority (NCA)

Focuses on complaints relating to Mobile Network Operators (unauthorized deductions, SIM fraud, unwanted calls).

Toll-Free 0800-110-622
General Helpline 0307-011-419
Email complaints@nca.org.gh
